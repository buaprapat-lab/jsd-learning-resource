# Cohorts

This is the community layer of this curriculum. Each cohort of learners, instructors, and mentors has its own folder. Inside, each person keeps a `.md` file — a place to share resources, links, and recommendations that have helped them through the curriculum or their career in software development.

**If you're a learner:** find your cohort folder and open your file. Add anything you've found genuinely useful — a video that made closures click, a blog post that explained REST better than the docs, a tool that saved you hours. Format doesn't matter much; you'll see an example in the existing files.

**If you're an instructor or mentor:** add your file too. What you've found valuable over years of practice is often more useful to learners than any curated list.

To add yourself, copy [`_template/cohort-member.md`](../_template/cohort-member.md) into your cohort folder and rename it to your name.
